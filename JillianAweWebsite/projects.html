Logic and Digital Design - Line Following Robo-Car

As a final project for Logic and Digital Design, I designed and built a line following Robo-Car using physical logic gates, IR obstacle detection sensors, line detection sensors, and a motor driver. The car was designed to pass beginner, intermediate, and advanced tracks, with iterative improvements such as additional sensors, PWM speed control, and reverse motor logic to optimize performance.

Circuit Modeling 2 - Transient Analysis of RLC Circuit

Perfboard RLC circuit used for transient analysis lab.

As a final lab project for Circuit Modeling 2, I performed a transient analysis of my own RLC Circuit. I was given a desired resonant frequency for the circuit and had to calculate the necessary resistor, inductor, and capacitor values to achieve this frequency. The circuit was then built on a perfboard and tested using a function generator and oscilloscope to observe the transient response and ensure that the response of the circuit matched theoretical predictions.
