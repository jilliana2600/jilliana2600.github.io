Extracurricular Activities

My involvement outside the classroom has helped me develop leadership, teamwork, communication, and hands-on technical skills that complement my engineering coursework.

Through Pioneer Robotics, I work on designing, building, and testing competition robots for VEX U competitions. My focus has included sensor integration, soldering custom components, and documenting the design process.

  • Soldered and tested custom sensors for odometry
  • Coordinated multiple teams and managed competition logistics as VEX U Coordinator

Society of Women Engineers (SWE)

  • Collaborated with faculty and student organizations
  • Promoted inclusivity and professional development in engineering
